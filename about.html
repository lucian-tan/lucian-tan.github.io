About Lucian

Born and raised in Singapore, I studied industrial design and moved into designing digital experiences for start-ups. I conduct user and market research, collaborate cross-functionally, and deliver designs in high fidelity to help businesses reach their objectives. I have shipped multiple features in different products and worked on improving design operations.

If I am not exploring a foreign city or hiking in the wilds, you can find me trying to do handstands, finding gastronomical delights in town, or chasing the next sunset to photograph.
